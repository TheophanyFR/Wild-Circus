Welcome to the Wild Circus!

Do you want to experience an unforgettable show ? Then look no further, embark with us on a journey filled with laugh, joy and amazement. Our circus is composed of performers from all horizons, a family sharing the same passion : to offer you a show you will remember.

Our acrobats, clowns and dancers will steal your breath away ! The Wild Circus travels worldwide, so we will probably make a stop in your country and we would love to see you there!

Below on this page you can have a sneak peek at the acts composing our current show. We wish you a pleasant visit on our website.
